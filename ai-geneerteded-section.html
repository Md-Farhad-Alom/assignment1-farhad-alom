Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers to help you prepare for DevConf 2026.

Will the workshop recordings be available after the event?

Yes! Depending on your ticket type, Standard ticket holders get 30 days of access, while Pro and Team ticket holders get lifetime access to all recorded sessions.

Can I upgrade my ticket from Standard to Pro later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade your ticket anytime before August 15, 2026, by visiting your profile dashboard or contacting our support team.

Is there a group discount available for teams?

Yes, our Team package covers up to 10 people at a heavily discounted rate. If you have a larger group, please use the "Contact Us" button to request a custom quote.
